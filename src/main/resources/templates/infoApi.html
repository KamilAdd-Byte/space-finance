Info about functionality

Space Finance is an application designed for couples who would like to have an insight into their expenses home-type shopping, delivery lunches or trip planning.

The view layer is supported by thymeleaf and bootstrap 4 and CSS shaping GUI. This application is programmed for two people (couple). User adds a receipt (fields: price, place and date operation).
